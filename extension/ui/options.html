About PII Checker

Privacy-first PII protection for LLM interfaces

Version: 1.0.0

This extension helps protect your personally identifiable information (PII) when using AI chat interfaces by detecting and masking sensitive data before it's sent.

Privacy Notice: All processing happens locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.
